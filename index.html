About

You can view the location of street trees throughout New York City via the filters on the right sidebar while the buttons along the bottom load different layers.

The views are generated from data provided by the NYC Parks Department.

The data was cleaned, munged, analyzed and presented by our team at a DataKind event held in September 2012 at SVA.

Species info from Encyclopedia of Life.
